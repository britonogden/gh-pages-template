    Strategic Aerial Media utilizes remotely-operated quadcopters to capture full high-definition video and photos. All aerial flights are for recreation purposes only, while we await implementation of FAA regulations concerning UAV's. We fly in accordance with our own, self imposed, UAV pilot code of conduct.

    1) Safety First. Never endanger others by flying in close proximity to them.

    2)Respect the Privacy of others. This means never infringing on others right or request to privacy, even when there is no expectation of privacy, such as in a public place.

    3) Always fly in a manner that avoids interference with airports or other aircraft
